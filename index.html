About Me

I double majored in Finance and Management Information Systems (MIS). My double major led me to a career as an Operations Manager in Financial Services. I have been working for Interactive Brokers LLC (Ticker: IBKR) since I graduated from Villanova. As an Operations Manager for a heavily automated firm, I understand the importance of technology in the day-to-day operations of our industry.


I hope to combine my hobbies with the knowledge I gain from the coding bootcamp. Perhaps I can even leverage this knowledge to create a business of my own one day!
